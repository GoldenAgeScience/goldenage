About

Where Science Meets Spirit

Dr Hari Krishna is a medical doctor (MD) based in Bengaluru who has devoted more than 25 years to the study of consciousness and inner exploration. A rare bridge between the clinic and the cave, he presents spirituality not as blind belief but as a practical science — explaining the timeless truths of the Upanishads through the language of biology, neurology, psychology and quantum mechanics.

He teaches meditation as a lifestyle, not an escape: a practical transformation that dissolves fear and anxiety while expanding compassion and awareness. Those who learn from him speak of his humility, his patience, and a selfless way of teaching that makes the deepest concepts simple for the modern mind.

The Physician

An MD with a Master's in Medicine — decades of clinical practice grounded in evidence and care.

The Seeker — 25+ Years

A parallel journey inward: meditation, the Upanishads, and the science of consciousness.
