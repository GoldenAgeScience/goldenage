About

Where Science Meets Spirit

Dr Hari Krishna is a medical doctor (MD) based in Bengaluru who has devoted more than 25 years to the study of consciousness and inner exploration. A rare bridge between the clinic and the cave, he presents spirituality not as blind belief but as a practical science — explaining the timeless truths of the Upanishads, Aham Brahmasmi and Tat Tvam Asi, through the language of biology, neurology, psychology and quantum mechanics.

He teaches meditation as a lifestyle, not an escape: a practical transformation that dissolves fear and anxiety while expanding compassion and awareness. Genuine awakening, he reminds seekers, unfolds naturally through sincere practice — never through forced techniques. Those who learn from him speak of his humility, his patience, and a selfless way of teaching that makes the deepest concepts simple for the modern mind.
